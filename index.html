Work

After I graduated from college, I started off working in a factory that printed magazines for big companies (like Cabela's and National Geographic). A job opportunity opened at a local Christian school and I was hired as the middle and high school Bible teacher. As time wore on, some shifting of personel happened and I was asked to be the middle and high school computer teacher. This question did not come by chance since I was attempting to use technology in my classroom as much as I could.

When I took over the classes, the previous teacher had students use Khan Academy for programming. It was then that I became really interested in the abilities of building websites. I bought a couple of online classes and dove in. Ever since then, I decided to have my students at least learn how to build a website with HTMl and CSS. I've always been a little shaky on Javascript... but who hasn't, right?

After a few years, I discovered the ability to post your website to Github for free... which, as a teacher, is AWESOME! So we started adding our websites to Github last year so family and friends could see them. It's been really awesome! Every year, the students continue to improve and build amazing websites.

And this is where I've landed. I'm upset that my past websites don't exist today. I will continue to search for them, but I love doing this project with my students. I hope you enjoy checking out these websites. If so, drop me a line on twitter.
